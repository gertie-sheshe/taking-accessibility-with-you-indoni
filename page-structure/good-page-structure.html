Taking Accessibility With You

Understanding your user.

I will highlight how important it is to understand the different types of users before kicking off a project. This talk will show that users use the web differently in different scenarios, that there are users who are temporarily disabled and those that are fully disabled and so they access content using assistive technologies.

Design

This section will focus on the importance of simplicity in design and layout of your website for easier consumption of content. It will also stress on the importance of color contrast, accessible forms, how to better do data visualisation and what font sizes make your website easier to access.

Development

In this section, I will take the audience through how to develop the site in an accessible manner. I will talk about how to use semantic mark up, how to make accessible custom elements, how to use ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) attributes and best practices to follow for links, images and tabbing order. This part will have code samples and demos.
